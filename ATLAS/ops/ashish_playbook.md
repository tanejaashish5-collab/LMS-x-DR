## What YOU Do (10 Minutes Every Morning)

### Step 1: Check the Dashboard (2 min)

Go to: **https://lms-x-dr.vercel.app/dashboard**

Look at three things:

1. **Budget** tab -- make sure you are not burning through cash. Monthly limit is $250.
2. **Opportunities** tab -- how many new leads Scout discovered.
3. **Pipeline** tab -- any deals moving forward?

If budget is above $200 remaining and opportunities are coming in, everything is healthy. Move on.


### Step 2: Check Pipeline for Warm Leads (3 min)

Go to: **https://lms-x-dr.vercel.app/dashboard/pipeline**

The pipeline has 8 stages. You only care about two:

| Stage | What It Means | Your Action |
|-------|--------------|-------------|
| **Responded** | SOMEONE REPLIED to an email. This is money. | Read their reply. Respond personally. |
| **Negotiating** | Active conversation happening. | Keep emailing them. Book a call if they are interested. |

Ignore these -- CLOSER handles them automatically:
- Discovered (new finds)
- Qualified (scored high enough)
- Proposed (proposal sent, waiting for reply)

Also ignore:
- Closed Won / Closed Lost (done deals)
- Delivering (already building)


### Step 3: Reply to Warm Leads (5 min)

If someone moved to "Responded":

1. Open your email: **ashish@forgevoice.studio**
2. Read their reply
3. Respond personally -- be human, be helpful, no copy-paste
4. If they want to talk, send your Calendly: **https://calendly.com/ashishtaneja**
5. Book a 15-minute call

**That is it. Go live your life.**

## When a Lead Replies -- What to Write Back

Keep it short. Three sentences max. Match their energy.

**If they are interested:**
> Thanks [Name] -- glad this landed at the right time. Happy to jump on a quick 15-min call to see if what we do is a fit. Here is my calendar: [Calendly link]

**If they have questions:**
> Great question. [Answer their specific question in 1-2 sentences]. Want to jump on a quick call? Easier to show than explain: [Calendly link]

**If they say "not now":**
> No worries at all. I will check back in a month. If anything changes before then, just hit reply.

"Hi [Name], thanks for getting in touch. I am Ashish from ForgeVoice Studio.

I saw you are looking for help with [their pain point -- check the opportunity data on the dashboard].

Explain three things:
1. **What they get** -- a custom landing page or automation workflow, not a template
2. **How fast** -- 3-5 days for Starter, 5-7 for Pro, 7-14 for Enterprise
3. **The price** -- start with the package closest to what they described needing

"We can start building this week. I will send you a detailed proposal within 24 hours with exactly what you will get and the timeline.

Which package sounds closest to what you need -- the Starter at $997 or the Professional at $2,497?"

### If They Say "Let Me Think About It"

"Totally understand. I will send the proposal now so you have everything in writing. It includes the exact timeline, what is included, and a sample of what we would build for you. No pressure at all."

### If They Ask "Why So Cheap?"

"Great question. Traditional agencies use teams of designers and developers which takes weeks and costs thousands. We use proprietary AI that does 90% of the heavy lifting in hours, not weeks. You get the same quality at a fraction of the cost and time."

## Troubleshooting -- If Something Looks Wrong

### No new opportunities appearing
Scout runs Mon/Wed/Fri at 5am AEST. If it is Tuesday and nothing new has appeared since Monday, that is normal. If nothing appears for a full week, tell Claude to check the Scout agent logs on Railway.

### Budget showing $0 or very low
Vault resets monthly. If budget is low mid-month, ATLAS automatically enters conservation mode (only high-value actions). It will recover at month start.

### Pipeline stuck -- leads not moving
CLOSER processes pipeline daily at 7am. If a lead has been in "Proposed" for 14+ days with no response, CLOSER will auto-close it. This is normal.

### Dashboard not loading
Try: https://lms-x-dr.vercel.app/dashboard directly. If it is down, Vercel may be having issues. Wait 10 minutes and try again. If it persists, tell Claude to check the Vercel deployment.

### Need to pause ATLAS
Go to Railway (railway.app), find the ATLAS service, and click "Pause". This stops all agents. To restart, click "Deploy" or "Restart".
